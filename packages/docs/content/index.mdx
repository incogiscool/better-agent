# Everything you need to ship your agent

BetterAgent gives your SaaS an AI assistant that does real work inside the
product you already shipped. Point it at your codebase — it reads your routes
and server actions, generates the schemas, and drops in the chat components.

These docs cover the full path: a step-by-step **Quickstart**, the **CLI
reference**, the **tool file** schema that teaches your agent what it can do,
and the **component registry** for the chat UI.

## Prefer your AI IDE?

Skip the manual steps. Copy the [AI setup prompt](/ai-setup) into Claude Code,
Cursor, or any AI coding assistant and it installs the packages, authenticates,
discovers your tools, and wires up the provider for you.
